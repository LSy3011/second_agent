# Knowledge Workflow Skill

## Purpose

Plan a reusable enterprise knowledge workflow from a user's documents,
domain knowledge, tools, and target business task.

## Best For

- R&D knowledge assistants
- Technical research workflows
- Internal document Q&A
- Sales engineering knowledge support
- Enterprise training and onboarding assistants
- Agentic RAG architecture planning

## Inputs

- Available knowledge sources
- Target users or teams
- Expected task
- Existing tools or services
- Reliability, privacy, and deployment constraints

## Output Structure

1. Business scenario and users
2. Knowledge service design
3. Memory and personalization design
4. Tool/MCP integration plan
5. Skill workflow design
6. Reliability and evaluation checklist

## Rules

- Treat job-search or interview preparation as optional validation examples,
  not as the core product background.
- Prefer enterprise-facing scenarios such as R&D, sales engineering, internal
  training, technical support, and document intelligence.
- Connect each component to a clear responsibility: knowledge source, retrieval,
  memory, tool call, workflow, evaluation, and deployment.
- Avoid unverifiable metrics. Mark assumptions clearly.
